# 🎒 Student Notes & Question Banks

Welcome to the **Student Notes** hub at Tech Nexus!

Education should be free and accessible to everyone. This learning center provides structured academic support for K-12 students, offering high-quality revision materials, textbook solutions, and exam preparation resources.

## 🗂️ Resource Directory

Navigate to your required study materials below:

### 1. [Class 6–12 Study Notes](./class-6-12/)
Comprehensive, chapter-wise revision notes for core subjects.
* **Subjects:** Mathematics, Science (Physics, Chemistry, Biology), Computer Science, and English.
* **Format:** Easy-to-read markdown files with bullet points, important formulas, and highlighted key terms.

### 2. [NCERT Solutions](./ncert-solutions/)
Step-by-step solutions to textbook exercises.
* **Focus:** Helping students understand the *how* and *why* behind an answer, rather than just providing the final result.
* **Organization:** Categorized by Class, then Subject, then Chapter.

### 3. [Question Banks & Past Papers](./question-banks/)
Test your knowledge with real exam scenarios.
* **Resources:** Previous year question papers, sample mock tests, and competitive exam preparation (e.g., Olympiads, foundational JEE/NEET notes).
* **Solutions:** Included at the bottom of each question bank for self-assessment.

## 🤝 How to Contribute to Student Notes

We need educators, high school students, and subject matter experts to help build this out!

1. **Accuracy is Key:** Please double-check your facts, formulas, and spelling before submitting. Students will rely on this for their exams.
2. **Formatting:** Use headings (`#`, `##`), bold text for definitions, and bullet points to make the notes easy to scan on a mobile phone.
3. **Use LaTeX for Math:** When writing complex math or physics formulas, please format them clearly so they render correctly on GitHub.

Please read our master [Contributing Guidelines](../../CONTRIBUTING.md) before submitting a Pull Request!
